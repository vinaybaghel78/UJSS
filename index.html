Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha

Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha is a non-profitable trust which is providing support and focus focus on activities in areas involving health, education, infrastructure, advocacy of minority rights and support of the poor.
The vision of Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha is to inspire students with specified knowledge, skills, motivated & passionate about themselves and values to enable them to become good human beings, socially responsible citizens.

Messages



This is a school for remote rural area in my mother's village in India where there were no schools around for thousands of years.
Now we have approx 450 children since 1996 studying upto 12th standard. This year, 2019, we have more than 90% pass Result for 10th & 12th Board Exams conducted by State Govt. Credit goes to the teachers, students, parents and my wife (who guides on school education system).



Trust President - Mr Omveer Singh Baghel

Our schools mission is to prepare young people to become active, engaged and responsible citizens of the local and global community. Highest quality teaching is a critical component of our school’s Strategic Plan. In our curriculum the student is the primary focus and each child is involved in the learning process. We are constantly improving our teaching methodology while academic excellence is our major thrust.Our aim to provide excellence in all our education programs and we care for our students with the best opportunity to achieve success. We are certain that you will find Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha to be an excellent choice for your children growth and success.



Principal - Mr Mukesh Arya
